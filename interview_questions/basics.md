1. Tell me about yourself?

SIMPLE VERSION TO INTRODUCE YOURSELF IS :

“Good morning sir/ma’am. My name is [Name]. I’ve recently completed my [Degree] in [Branch] from [College]. During my studies, I worked on a project on [project topic], where I gained hands-on experience with [key skills/tools]. I also completed [internship/course/activity], which helped me improve my problem-solving and teamwork skills. I’m interested in the role of [role/field] because I enjoy working on [relevant interest], and I’m looking forward to starting my career in a company where I can learn, contribute, and grow.”

IN SHORTER WE CAN SAY LIKE:

“Good morning sir/ma’am. I’m [Name], a [Branch] graduate from [College]. I’ve worked on a project related to [topic] and gained experience in [skills]. I’m interested in [role] and excited to learn and grow in a professional environment.”
